About CSRs

What are CSRs?

A Certificate Signing Request (CSR) contains your public key and information about your organization. You submit it to an external CA (like DigiCert or Let's Encrypt) to get a signed certificate while keeping your private key secure locally.

Workflow:

  1. Create CSR with encrypted private key
  2. Download and submit CSR to external CA
  3. Receive signed certificate from CA
  4. Import signed certificate back into HomeLab PKI

CSR Status:

  • Pending - Awaiting signed certificate
  • Signed - Certificate imported
  • Expired - CSR no longer valid
